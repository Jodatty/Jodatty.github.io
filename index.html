How much does a custom frameless shower door cost in Charlotte?

We quote most projects directly from photos you send us — even rough framing or elevation drawings, before tile is installed. See our full process for details. Final measure happens after we’ve agreed on a price in principle, and it rarely changes the number. That means you get an accurate price fast, without waiting on a technician visit and days of processing.

Is low-iron glass the same as Starphire or Starfire glass?

Yes, essentially. Low iron glass (also called crystal clear or ultra clear glass) removes the greenish tint found in standard tempered glass. Starphire® and Starfire® are two of the most recognized brand names for low-iron glass in the industry. We offer both standard clear and low-iron glass depending on your project and budget.

How long does a shower door installation take?

Once glass is fabricated to your final measurements, installation is typically completed in a single visit. Glass is ordered after measurement and arrives within five to ten business days depending on the configuration.

Do I need a contractor's license to install a shower door in North Carolina?

North Carolina does not require a general contractor's license for projects under $40,000. Lincoln Shower Door is operated by a glass fabrication and installation professional with over 20 years of industry experience.

Do you work with contractors or homeowners?

Both. We frequently partner with general contractors and builders throughout the Charlotte metro area, as well as working directly with homeowners.
